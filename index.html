About Us

Learn more about our company, values, and commitment to quality

Haroon and Sons is a leading export and import company based in Pakistan, specializing in high-quality duck down, duck feathers, and animal feed. With a rich legacy of trust and reliability, we have built a solid reputation in the global market.


Our goal is to provide superior products, exceptional customer service, and to ensure long-lasting relationships with clients across the world. We maintain rigorous quality control measures to ensure that all our products meet international standards.


With decades of experience in the industry, we understand the specific needs of our clients and work diligently to meet and exceed their expectations. Our team of experts ensures that every shipment is handled with care and precision.

Our Vision & Mission

Our guiding principles that drive our business forward

Our Vision

To become the preferred supplier of premium quality duck down, feathers, and animal feed, recognized for our commitment to excellence, timely deliveries, and customer satisfaction. We aim to expand our global footprint while maintaining the highest standards of quality and reliability.
